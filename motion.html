What is Motion?

Definition: Motion is the change in the position of an object with time.

Types of Motion

  • Rectilinear Motion: Movement in a straight line. Example: a ball rolling straight.
  • Circular Motion: Movement in a circular path. Example: Earth around the Sun.
  • Oscillatory Motion: Back-and-forth movement. Example: a swing or pendulum.

Quick Example Problem

Q: Identify the type of motion for a cyclist moving on a straight road.

A: Rectilinear motion.

Summary

Motion is everywhere — from clock hands to planets. Understanding types of motion helps in solving many physics problems.
