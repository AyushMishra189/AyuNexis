Motion (Class 9)

Motion is the change in position of an object with time.

Types of Motion

  • Rectilinear Motion: Movement along a straight line. Example: Ball rolling straight.
  • Circular Motion: Movement along a circular path. Example: Wheel spinning.
  • Oscillatory Motion: Back-and-forth movement. Example: Swing.
